BATMAN

Batman

Gotham's enigmatic protector, Batman relies on his intellect, martial arts prowess, and cutting-edge technology. With a tragic past motivating his mission, he stands as a vigilant guardian against crime and corruption.

Superman

An iconic Kryptonian, Superman boasts superhuman strength, speed, flight, heat vision, and invulnerability. As a symbol of hope, he fights for truth and justice, defending Earth from supervillains and global threats.

Flash

Forensic scientist Barry Allen taps into the Speed Force, granting him super speed and time manipulation. He dashes through challenges and villains, seeking answers while embracing heroism's responsibility.

Aquaman

Arthur Curry, the Atlantean king, harnesses aquatic might, commanding sea creatures and controlling water itself. Balancing his dual heritage, he defends the oceans and land against both human and Atlantean threats.

Shazam

Billy Batson, a young boy, transforms into Shazam by invoking the magic word. Shazam embodies six legendary figures' powers, granting him superhuman strength, speed, wisdom, and more. He retains the appearance of an adult while possessing the heart and innocence of a child.
